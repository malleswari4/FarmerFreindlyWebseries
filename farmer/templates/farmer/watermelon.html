INFORMATION ON WATERMELON

Watermelons are mainly cultivated in Maharashtra, Karnataka, Tamil Nadu, Punjab, Rajasthan, Madhya Pradesh, and Uttar Pradesh.

  • It contain 92% water along with proteins, minerals and carbohydrates.
  • Watermelon grows well in deep fertile and well-drained soil.
  • pH of soil should be in between 6-7

    • Time of sowing

  • In north it is sown during middle of January to March and in November to December.

    • Method of sowing For sowing different methods of planting like

  • furrow method
  • Pit method
    • hill method can be used depending upon climate and season.

  • Seed Rate
  • For sowing one acre land, seed rate of 1.5 to 2 kg is required.

    • Seed Treatment

    Before sowing treat seed with Carbendazim@2gm/kg of seeds. After chemical treat seeds with Trichoderma viride@4gm per kg of seeds. Dry seeds in shade and then do sowing immediately.

    Harvesting

    If tendril near stem gets dried also whitish color of fruit which touch to ground get yellowish then assume that fruit is ready for harvesting
