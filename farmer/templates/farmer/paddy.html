Information on Paddy Crop

Rice is the seed of a grass variety called Oryza sativa and Oryza glaberrima. Climate for Paddy Cultivation It also needs bright sunshine with temperature ranging between 20 and 400C It can tolerate temperature upto 420C

Season for Rice Crop

India has three rice farming seasons- summer, autumn and winter.

  • ‘kharif’ season also called ‘winter rice’.
  • June-July and is harvested during November- December months
  • rabi season is also called as ‘summer rice'
  • November to February and harvested during March to June
  • The pre-kharif or ‘autumn rice’ is sown during May to August
